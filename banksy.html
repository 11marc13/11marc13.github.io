Artist Catalog: Banksy

Artist Catalog: Banksy

This is a book of curated pieces by Banksy. The oversized hand-assembled book is 13" wide and 10" tall and has a total of 44 pages. The copy throughout the book is from three different interviews Banksy has given and even though he wasn't talking about the art specifically, the pieces I chose for each page connects with the messaging and theme of the copy. While there is an underlying grid system, images and copy are placed asymmetrically to connect with Banksy's idological view of society. Below are some spreads, including the cover.
