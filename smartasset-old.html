SmartAsset

SmartAsset is a fintech company specializing in connecting users with financial advisors in their platform. That said, for a majority of my time there I was the only marketing designer so I worked on a range of projects; including web design, B2C ads for a variety of marketing channels and B2B marketing collateral.

Web Design

The landing pages were only accessible by clicking on an ad a user comes across. The main purpose of these designs was to increase CVR on our content marketing advertorial landing pages. For the redesign, it was important to establish a universal template for a library of SmartAsset articles while also allowing for more custom visualizations depending on the copy. Elements like the new hero helped contribute to increased CVR performance across other pages, including a different one I redesigned that achieved a 140% increase in CVR.
